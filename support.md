# Screenshot Alpha Support

Thank you for using Screenshot Alpha.

Screenshot Alpha is a macOS application designed to make taking screenshots and recording your screen simple and efficient.

## Need Help?

If you experience a problem with Screenshot Alpha, please contact us by email.

**Email:** [sreekrishnanyadav@gmail.com](mailto:sreekrishnanyadav@gmail.com)

When contacting support, please include:

* A brief description of the issue
* Your macOS version
* The version of Screenshot Alpha you are using
* A screenshot of the issue, if applicable

We will do our best to respond as soon as possible.

## Privacy

Screenshot Alpha processes screenshots and screen recordings locally on your Mac. For more information, please see our Privacy Policy.
