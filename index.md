# Privacy Policy for Screenshot Alpha

**Last Updated: July 7, 2026**

Screenshot Alpha respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Screenshot Alpha application for macOS.

## Data Collection

Screenshot Alpha does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The app does not require users to create an account or provide personal information.

## Screenshots and Screen Recordings

All screenshots and screen recordings created using Screenshot Alpha are processed and stored locally on your Mac.

Screenshot Alpha does not upload your screenshots, screen recordings, images, videos, or other files to any external server.

You have full control over the files you create and save using the app.

## Permissions

Screenshot Alpha may request macOS permissions, including Screen Recording permission, to provide its core screenshot and screen-recording features.

These permissions are used only to enable the app's functionality. The app does not use these permissions to collect or transmit your data.

## Third-Party Services

Screenshot Alpha does not use third-party advertising or analytics services that collect personal data.

## Data Sharing

Screenshot Alpha does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

## Children's Privacy

Screenshot Alpha does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or Screenshot Alpha's privacy practices, please contact the developer through the support information provided on the App Store.
